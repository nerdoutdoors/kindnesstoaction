Our story

Elijah died tragically at 14. In his stead, we ask his generation and us, their parents, to turn kindness into action. Elijah cared deeply for fairness, equality, justice, and fun. With an engineer's mindset, he wanted to solve the world's problems. Permanently. He once said that he didn't want to hug a tree or save a whale, he wanted to make it so no one ever has to hug a tree or save a whale again. It all starts with kindness.
